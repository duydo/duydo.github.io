About Me

I’m Duy Do. I live in Saigon, Vietnam with my wife and two cute kids. I’m a passionate software engineer with solid background in computer science, more than 8 years of experience in building and scaling applications.

My areas of interest include information retrieval, distributed systems, streaming data processing, data analytics, big data, performance monitoring and development of new products in new and interesting ways.

I love open sources. Recently, I have been contributing to Elasticsearch with Vietnamese analysis plugin and founded the Vietnamese Elasticsearch community.
